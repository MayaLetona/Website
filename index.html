About the Creators

We are three high school students that have a variety of skill in computer science but what the three of us have in common is wanting to help others while using computer science. In doing so we also want to change the gender inequality of women in engineering and computer science. We came to a program called Girls Who Code to become leaders and create a sisterhood with other girls that have a similar passion in computer science and female empowerment.
